# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Keep technical disagreement focused on the work, not the person.

## Expected behavior

- Give actionable feedback.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep reviews factual and direct.
- Respect maintainers' time and release constraints.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or abuse
- Personal attacks
- Bad-faith derailment
- Publishing private security details publicly

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when behavior damages collaboration.
